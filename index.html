Why Code?

Why? I've wanted to learn to code for as long as I can remember. Being able to make games, applications, and webpages seemed like it would be an amazing career! For a long time, I thought it was out of my reach, but then a friend told me about Epicodus. Working through the program has its ups and downs, but overall its made what felt unattainable feel possible. I'm looking forward to pairing with the rest of the cohort, and learning from them as much as I can.
